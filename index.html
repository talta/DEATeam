Who we support

What is your work's digital edge?:

Are you running out of bandwidth, tracking for a reduction in revenue, or nearing a growth point that is unscalable? Do you have too many projects and too few systems that can effectively manage the workflow?

Whether your team is at the edge now, or if you’re looking ahead and realize they will be, Digital Edge Advisors can help you make the leap and land on solid ground.

Consider working with us if any of these situations ring true for your work |

  • Software has tech debt that is preventing the tech and business from growing to realize benefits of newer technology
  • Tech teams don’t know where to start with long list of projects and broken systems
  • Business teams are upset that delivery is taking too long for key projects
  • Business teams have a long list of high priority projects
  • Projects start and end without delivering value
  • Platforms are purchased without being implemented
  • Unable to expand into Europe due to GDPR restrictions
  • Identified need for more people in IT, but but budget doesn't exist
  • Previous digital transformations have failed
  • Revenue is decreasing and production bugs exist
  • Newly appointed CIO needs support to fix systems without change fatigue
  • 3rd party contracts are signed without negotiations for best prices
  • Products are purchased without fit analysis, nor buy and build decisions
